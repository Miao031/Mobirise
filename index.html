INTRODUCTION

随着时代的发展，人们由最初的线下消费逐渐转移到线上消费，商品也由实际物品转换为图片，而这些图片转换为我们是否对它进行购买，我们年轻人对这种网购的接受程度是比较快的，但是我们的爷爷奶奶长时间习惯于线下消费，因而他们在线上消费的操作有可能是比较困难的，无法购买到想要购买的物品，而且大部分人群具有选择困难症，如何助力消费选择呢？因此我们希望设计一个智能选择的机器帮助用户实现网购选择，通过所设计的交互产品为用户制造网购选择消费者的幻觉。

Along with the development of The Times, people from the initial offline consumption gradually transferred to online consumption, commodities have the actual items into images, those images can be converted into us whether to buy it, our young people to the acceptance of online shopping is relatively fast, but our grandma and grandpa used to offline for a long time consumption, thus they operate online consumption is likely to be more difficult, cannot buy to want to buy items, but most people choose difficult disease, how to choose power consumption? Therefore, we hope to design an intelligent selection machine to help users realize online shopping selection, and create the illusion of online shopping selection consumers for users through the designed interactive products.

Function

Online shopping power glasses scan pictures through a circular camera below.Glasses are selected through speech recognition, scanned images, and object recognition.

Materials

The material selects the light and thin material, creates the air feeling, in some parts, we also chose the aluminum alloy. Because it's an eye product, we hope it's lighter, and there's no pressure and pressure to wear it.

Color

Considering the eyesight of the elderly, we refer to the color of the life buoy for the color of the glasses. Bright colors that can be easily found are shown in the design drawings.

Aims

Combined with the purchasing power and physical fitness of users, select the function that meets the characteristics of the experience of choice phobia. It can be used as an assistant to choose online shopping for people with phobia.

1

Choice

How can designers leverage the uses of consumers'choices to
create optimal experience?
  
Online shopping for the elderly is a process in which the elderly themselves take the initiative to adapt to the information society, which is not easy for the elderly. The designer needs to make the design more user-friendly and give more consideration to the practicality and functionality of the design. In combination with the purchasing power and physical quality of the elderly, we choose to design functions that conform to the experience characteristics of the elderly. In contrast, the appearance design needs to be simple for the public, which can be appreciated by young people as the assistant of online shopping for the elderly. It creates an illusion that consumers actually choose online shopping when they buy online. 

2

Illusion

Our final project is presented in the form of modeling and product diagram. Through the data provided by the product, we guide consumers to choose according to the consumption plan provided, giving them the illusion of “independent choice of consumption”. However, in fact, we cannot completely get rid of the consumption plan provided by the machine.
